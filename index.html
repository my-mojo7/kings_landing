About Us

The King's Landing is the most versatile club in Bermuda and praises itself for not just gaining revenue during hours of darkness but also during daylight. King's Landing is located in a two-story building and makes use of both floors. The first floor hosts a restaurant/sports bar and pool tables which is open day and night.

During the night, the second floor strictly for dancing, however, during daytime operations the second floor is used as a space that is rented out to entrepreneurs or event organizers.
